# About ShArc

**ShArc** is a geoscience simulation application for **porous media two-phase flow**, built on the [Arcane framework](https://github.com/arcaneframework).

## Background

ShArc is developed by **CEA** (Commissariat à l'énergie atomique et aux énergies alternatives) and **IFPEN** (IFP Energies nouvelles) for simulating complex multiphase flow in geological porous media.

## Capabilities

- **Two-Phase Flow Simulation** — Water/gas flow with Newton-Krylov solvers, capillary pressure, relative permeability, and well models
- **CAWF** — Compaction/Elasticity/FEM solvers for geomechanical simulations
- **ExaDiBench** — Mesh geometry benchmarks for exascale co-design
